How to Make an Fried Egg Bagel


A delicious breakfast bagel, with an fried style egg inside and a bit of spice. Great quick meal for breakfast or lunch, takes 5-10 minutes in total.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 Bagel
  • Butter
  • Chilli Crisp

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan on medium-high heat
  2. Use butter to grease pan
  3. Crack egg into pan
  4. Add pinch of salt, pepper and chilli crisp
  5. Place a plate on top (steam will cook the top of the egg)
  6. Meanwhile, quickly butter your bagel
  7. Once egg is ready to your preference, place directly on bagel
  8. Serve and enjoy!
